## 📌 What Are Integrations?

Trackpilots integrations connect your workspace to popular communication platforms. When a team member switches between **Work Mode** and **Privacy Mode**, Trackpilots automatically sends an alert to your configured channel.

Currently supported platforms:

| Platform | Status |
|---|---|
| 💜 Slack | ✅ Available |
| 🔵 Microsoft Teams | ✅ Available |
| 🟣 Discord | ✅ Available |

---

## 🔔 What Gets Notified?

All three integrations support the same notification triggers:

| Trigger | Description |
|---|---|
| `work_privacy_mode_change` | Fires when any team member switches to Work Mode or Privacy Mode |

When enabled, your channel receives a message like:

> 🟢 **John Doe** switched to **Work Mode** — 17 May 2026, 09:30 AM
> 🔴 **Jane Smith** switched to **Privacy Mode** — 17 May 2026, 11:00 AM

---

## ⚙️ How to Connect

Each integration follows a similar flow:

1. Go to **[Integrations](https://app.trackpilots.com/integrations)** in your Trackpilots dashboard
2. Click **Connect** on your preferred platform
3. Authorize Trackpilots via OAuth
4. Select the channel where alerts should be delivered
5. Enable the notification rules you want
