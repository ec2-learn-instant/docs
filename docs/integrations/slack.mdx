💜 **Slack Integration**

Connect Trackpilots to Slack to automatically post work mode alerts into any channel in your workspace.

## 📌 Overview

The Slack integration allows Trackpilots to send real-time notifications to a Slack channel whenever a team member switches between **Work Mode** and **Privacy Mode**.

**What you'll need:**
- A Slack workspace where you have permission to install apps
- Admin or developer access in Trackpilots

---

## 🚀 How to Connect Slack

### Step 1 — Open Integrations

1. Log in to [app.trackpilots.com](https://app.trackpilots.com)
2. Navigate to **Integrations** in the left sidebar
3. Locate the **Slack** card and click **Connect**

### Step 2 — Authorize via OAuth

You will be redirected to Slack's authorization page.

1. Select the **Slack workspace** you want to connect
2. Choose the **channel** where Trackpilots should post alerts
3. Click **Allow**

Trackpilots requests only the `incoming-webhook` scope — it can only post to the selected channel and nothing else.

### Step 3 — Connection Confirmed

After authorization, you'll be redirected back to the Integrations page. The Slack card will show **Connected** status.

A welcome message is automatically posted to your chosen channel confirming the connection.

---

## 🔔 Notification Rules

Once connected, you can configure which events trigger Slack notifications.

| Rule | Trigger | Default |
|---|---|---|
| Work / Privacy Mode Change | When any member switches mode | Enabled |

To update rules:
1. Click **Manage** on the Slack integration card
2. Toggle the rules on or off
3. Click **Save Rules**

---

## 🧪 Test the Integration

After connecting, use the **Send Test Message** button on the Slack card to verify your connection. A test message will be posted to your configured channel immediately.

---

## 🔌 Disconnect Slack

To remove the Slack integration:

1. Go to **Integrations** → **Slack**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Confirm the action

This stops all Slack notifications. Your Slack workspace is not affected — Trackpilots is simply removed from the channel.

---

## 💬 Sample Notification

When a team member switches modes, your Slack channel receives:

```
🟢 John Doe switched to Work Mode — 17 May 2026, 09:30 AM
```

```
🔴 Jane Smith switched to Privacy Mode — 17 May 2026, 11:00 AM
```

<Callout type="warning">
⚠️ **Channel Permissions**

</Callout>
