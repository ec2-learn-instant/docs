## 📌 1. Introduction

**Trackpilots** is a modern employee monitoring and productivity analytics platform designed to help organizations manage workforce performance, optimize productivity, and ensure accountability across remote, hybrid, and in-office teams.

Trackpilots provides real-time insights into employee activity, time usage, application behavior, and productivity patterns, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions.

👉 Learn more: [https://trackpilots.com/](https://trackpilots.com/)

---

## ❓ 2. What is Trackpilots?

**Trackpilots** is a **workforce intelligence and productivity monitoring software** that tracks employee activities during work hours and generates actionable reports for managers, HR teams, and business owners.

It helps organizations:

* 📊 Measure employee productivity
* 👀 Monitor work activity in real-time
* ⏱️ Track time spent on tasks, apps, and websites
* 🧾 Generate automated attendance and productivity reports
* ⚙️ Identify inefficiencies and optimize workflows

Trackpilots is suitable for startups, SMBs, and enterprises.

👉 About Trackpilots: [https://trackpilots.com/about-us](https://trackpilots.com/about-us)

## ✨ 4. Key Features

### 🖥️ 4.1 Employee Activity Monitoring

Trackpilots monitors employee system activities during work hours, including:

* 🟢 Active and idle time tracking
* ▶️ Work session start and end events
* ☕ Break tracking
* 🧭 Activity timelines

👉 Feature page: [https://trackpilots.com/features/employee-monitoring-software](https://trackpilots.com/features/employee-monitoring-software)

---

### 📸 4.2 Screenshot Monitoring

Trackpilots captures periodic screenshots of employee screens to:

* ✅ Validate work activity
* 🔐 Improve transparency and compliance

Screenshots are configurable with privacy controls.

---

### 🌐 4.3 App and Website Usage Tracking

Trackpilots records:

* 🧩 Applications used by employees
* 🌍 Websites visited during work hours
* ⏳ Time spent on each app and website

Apps and websites can be categorized as **Productive, Neutral, or Unproductive**.

---

### ⏱️ 4.4 Time Tracking

Trackpilots automatically tracks:

* 🕒 Work hours
* 💤 Idle time
* ⚡ Active time
* 📌 Task-level time tracking (optional)
