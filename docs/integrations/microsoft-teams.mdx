🔵 **Microsoft Teams Integration**

Connect Trackpilots to Microsoft Teams to automatically post work mode alerts into any channel in your organization.
</Callout>

## 📌 Overview

The Microsoft Teams integration uses an **Incoming Webhook** connector to deliver Trackpilots notifications directly into a Teams channel. No app store approval required — it takes under two minutes to set up.

**What you'll need:**
- Microsoft Teams with permission to add connectors to a channel
- Admin or developer access in Trackpilots

---

## 🚀 How to Connect Microsoft Teams

### Step 1 — Create an Incoming Webhook in Teams

1. Open **Microsoft Teams** and go to the channel where you want alerts
2. Click the **⋯ (More options)** menu next to the channel name
3. Select **Connectors**
4. Search for **Incoming Webhook** and click **Configure**
5. Give it a name (e.g., `Trackpilots`) and optionally upload an icon
6. Click **Create**
7. **Copy the webhook URL** — you'll need this in Trackpilots

### Step 2 — Connect in Trackpilots

1. Log in to [app.trackpilots.com](https://app.trackpilots.com)
2. Navigate to **Integrations** in the left sidebar
3. Locate the **Microsoft Teams** card and click **Connect**
4. Paste the **Incoming Webhook URL** from Step 1
5. Click **Connect**

### Step 3 — Connection Confirmed

The Teams card will show **Connected** status. A welcome message is automatically posted to your Teams channel confirming the connection.

---

## 🔔 Notification Rules

Once connected, you can configure which events trigger Teams notifications.

| Rule | Trigger | Default |
|---|---|---|
| Work / Privacy Mode Change | When any member switches mode | Enabled |

To update rules:
1. Click **Manage** on the Teams integration card
2. Toggle the rules on or off
3. Click **Save Rules**

---

## 🧪 Test the Integration

After connecting, use the **Send Test Message** button on the Teams card to verify your connection. A test message will be posted to your configured channel immediately.

---

## 🔌 Disconnect Teams

To remove the Teams integration:

1. Go to **Integrations** → **Microsoft Teams**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Confirm the action

This stops all Teams notifications. The Incoming Webhook connector remains in Teams — you can delete it from the Teams connector settings if no longer needed.
