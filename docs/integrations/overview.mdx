## 📌 What Are Integrations?

Trackpilots integrations send automatic notifications to your team's communication channels. When someone switches to **Work Mode** or **Privacy Mode**, your connected channel gets an instant alert — no manual checking needed.

## 🚀 Get Started

Go to **[Integrations](https://app.trackpilots.com/integrations)** in your Trackpilots dashboard and click **Connect** on any platform to get started.
