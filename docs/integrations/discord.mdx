🟣 **Discord Integration**

Receive instant work mode and privacy mode alerts directly in your Discord channel.
</Callout>

---

## 🚀 How to Connect

1. Go to **[Integrations](https://app.trackpilots.com/integrations)** in your Trackpilots dashboard
2. Click **Connect to Discord**
3. Select the **Discord server** where you want alerts posted
4. Click **Authorize**
5. Back in Trackpilots, select the **channel** for alerts
6. Click **Connect**

The Discord card will show **Connected** status, and a welcome message will appear in your chosen channel.

---

## 🔔 Notification Alerts

Once connected, your Discord channel will receive a message whenever a team member switches modes:

```
🟢 John Doe switched to Work Mode — 17 May 2026, 09:30 AM
🔴 Jane Smith switched to Privacy Mode — 17 May 2026, 11:00 AM
```

---

## ⚙️ Manage Notifications

You can turn notification rules on or off at any time:

1. Go to **Integrations** → **Discord**
2. Toggle the rules on or off
3. Click **Save Rules**

---

## 🧪 Test the Connection

Click **Send Test Message** on the Discord card to send a test alert to your channel instantly.

---

## 🔌 Disconnect

Click **Disconnect** on the Discord card to stop all Discord notifications.
